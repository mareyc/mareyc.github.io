Todai Plaza

Located in Gardena, you'll find many delicious options for Asian cuisine in this plaza and the surrounding area. Recommendations include Akane, Shinsengumi, Boiling Point, Crab Shack, Korean Restaurant.

The perfect spot to while away the afternoon on a sunny day.

The Point

This relatively new shopping center opened in 2015 and features a variety of shops, restaurants, and an inviting outdoor plaza that will invite you to while away the afternoon on a sunny day. Pictured above is North Italia. Favorites include North Italia (pictured abovie), Mendocino Farms, True Food Kitchen and Hopdoddy Burger Bar.

Farmers Market

American • Torrance • Reviews

Farmers Market

The Southbay is no stranger to fresh fruits and veggies! The Torrance market features a year-round selection of California-grown fruits, nuts, vegetables, eggs, honey, baked goods, fish, cut flowers and nursery stock brought in each week from 60 California farms.

Mar'sel

Located inside the Terranea Resort in Palos Verdes, this restaurant was named one of LA's Best Outdoor Restaurants by Los Angeles Magazine in 2016. Features inventive twists on California cuisine with incredible views overlooking the Pacific Ocean.

Fishing with Dynamite

This sister restaurant to the popular Manhattan Beach Post specializes in a fresh selection of seafood. Named best Oyster Bar by Los Angeles Magazine in 2013.

Kang Ho Dong Baekjeong

One of the best choices around for Korean BBQ! The first location opened in Koreatown in 2011 and since expanded to 7 more locations. Banchans include cheesy corn, steamed egg, and kimchi pancake.

King's Hawaiian

Mostly recognized for its Hawaiian bread, King's Hawaiian is also a full restaurant and bakery. Many colorful cakes and tarts greet you from behind the glass counter. Pictured above is their popular paradise cake with 3 multi-color chiffon layers of passion fruit, guava, and lime.
