International Printing Museum

A dynamic museum devoted to bringing the history of books, printing, and the book arts to life. Read more >

They house an extensive collection of antique printing presses, including a replica of the Gutenberg press. Staff is friendly and the price of admission is $10.

Southbay Botanical Garden

Located in Palos Verdes, this 87 acre space was one of the first botanic gardens to be developed over a sanitary landfill. Read more >

A great way to spend half a day strolling the grounds and learning about the various plants in their collection, now numbering over 200,000. Price of admission is $9.

Wayfarer's Chapel

This unique church was designed by Lloyd Wright (Frank Lloyd Wright's son) in the late 1940s and was built between 1949 and 1951. Read more >

Known as the glass church, this chapel is nestled in a grove of towering redwood trees. It's open to the public so when a ceremony isn't in progress, you can step inside for a few moments of solitude.
