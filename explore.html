Palos Verdes Nature Reserve

Experience some of the most amazing views of the Pacific Ocean along various hiking trails in the Palos Verdes Nature Reserve which covers 1400 acres. Read more >

They range in level of difficulty, geography, and wildlife. Popular trails include the Burma Trail, Ocean Trail, and Abalone Cove Shoreline Park.

Marvin Braude Bike Trail

Did you know that the entire coast of California is a national monument? Read more >

The California Coastal National Monument that is! Now you'll have the chance to bike ride along some of this 22 mile paved bicycle path. The southern end of the trail starts in Torrance and stretches all the way to the Pacific Palisades.

Catalina Island

This island resort is just a hop, step, and skip away which makes it the place for a perfect weekend getaway! Read more >

Set sail from the port of Long Beach or San Pedro and enjoy the 1 hour ride. Highlights include ziplining, plentiful water activities, and touring the historical Casino.
